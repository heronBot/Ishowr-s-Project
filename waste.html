How It Works

Join our mission to keep the Himalayas pristine. Collect waste during your trek, bring it to our recycling points, and earn rewards!

1

Collect Waste

During your trek or tour, collect waste materials you find along the trails or bring waste from your own journey.

2

Sort & Deposit

Visit any of our recycling stations located at major stops and sort your waste into plastics, metals, paper, and others.

3

Earn Eco Points

Get eco points based on the type and amount of waste you bring. Track your contribution in your account dashboard.

4

Redeem Rewards

Use your eco points for discounts on treks, locally crafted souvenirs, or meals at our partner restaurants.
